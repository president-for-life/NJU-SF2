Hall：
1. 搜索所有影厅
	HallService.searchAllHall()

Movie:
1. 上架电影
	MovieService.addMovie(MovieForm addMovieForm)
2. 搜索所有影厅
	MovieService.searchAllMovie()
3. 根据关键词搜索电影
	MovieService.getMovieByKeyword(String keyword)
4. 批量下架电影
	MovieService.pullOfBatchOfMovie(MovieBatchOffForm movieBatchOffForm)
5. 更新电影信息
	MovieService.updateMovie(MovieForm updateMovieForm)
	
Schedule:
1. 添加排片信息
	ScheduleService.addSchedule(ScheduleForm scheduleForm)
2. 查询包括从起始时间开始的7天排片计划
	ScheduleService.searchScheduleSevenDays(int hallId, Date startDate)
3. 批量删除排片信息
	ScheduleService.deleteBatchOfSchedule(ScheduleBatchDeleteForm scheduleBatchDeleteForm)
4. 修改排片信息
	ScheduleService.updateSchedule(ScheduleForm scheduleForm)
5. 观众看到的排片信息
	ScheduleService.getScheduleView()
